Ask for feedback once, when it is actually worth asking. A card now appears in the sidebar footer for people who have been using OpenKnowledge for at least two weeks and have built up at least ten documents, inviting a quick good / not great rating with room for a message. It waits its turn behind the first-run checklist and any what's-new notice, and it starts collapsed to just the question, expanding only once you pick a rating. Answer it or close it and it never comes back on that device. Documents that OpenKnowledge manages for you, like installed skills and folder templates, do not count toward the ten.
